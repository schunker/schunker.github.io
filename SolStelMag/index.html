Solar and Stellar Magnetic Activity

20-22 May 2026, Newcastle, Australia

Goals of the Workshop

The workshop aims to:
i) provide an overview of current Australian research on solar and stellar magnetic activity and their consequences for Earth and planetary environments, and
ii) identify synergies across research areas to establish foundations for future collaboration.

This meeting will bring together researchers active in solar and stellar magnetic activity and its effects on the Earth and planets from across Australia. Throughout the workshop, dedicated discussion sessions will invite active participation from all attendees to identify connections between research domains. We will conclude by identifying overarching scientific questions that connect our research areas and discussing strategies to build a collaborative Australian research community to advance the field.

How to get there

Unfortunately there is no dedicated parking at the University’s city campus; only paid on-street parking (typically limited to 2–4 hours) and nearby parking garages are available.
