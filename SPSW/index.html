Solar Physics and Space Weather Connections

September 29th-30th 2022, Newcastle, Australia

Goals of the Workshop

This workshop is being held in conjunction with the Australian Space Research Conference.

The idea of the workshop is to
i) get an overview of the current research efforts towards understanding the components of space weather; from the Sunâ€™s magnetism, solar wind propagation, and the corresponding magnetospheric, ionospheric and ground-based impacts, as well as
ii) to identify connections between our research areas and the needs of space weather users.

Participation at the workshop is by invitation only, and will bring together key players in the different relevant areas in Australia. We will close by identifying the current open questions we think we could tackle together, and discuss avenues of research to make further progress.

This meeting has been made possible with support from the University of Newcastle's Women In Research 2022 Fellowship.
